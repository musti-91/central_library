The College of Computer Science and Information Technology Organizes a Course on Employment Basics.

College of Computer Science and Information Technology 03 Feb 2025
05 Comments 3K Views

The College of Computer Science and Information Technology, in collaboration with the Employment and Rehabilitation Unit, organized a training course titled "Employment Basics: From Writing Resumes to Job Interviews." The course, which will be delivered by Assistant Professor Dr. Ahmad Jalak Shakir, will last for three days starting from March 9, 2025. It aims to develop job search skills and how to prepare and write a professional resume, as well as the secrets to success in job interviews.

This course is part of the college's distinguished activities, highlighting the importance of the information presented in enhancing the professional skills of the participants and learning how to seize unexpected opportunities in the job market.

The College of Computer Science and Information Technology organizes a workshop on the importance of afforestation.

The College of Computer Science and Information Technology, in cooperation with the Diwan Affairs Division and the Psychological Counseling and Educational Guidance Unit, organized a workshop entitled "Afforestation: An Investment in Our Future." The workshop addressed the importance of afforestation in preserving the environment and achieving sustainability, as well as the role of afforestation in promoting economic development. It also addressed the challenges facing afforestation and proposed solutions to overcome them. This initiative was part of the National Afforestation Day activities, highlighting the role of afforestation as a pivotal step toward a more sustainable future.

Who We Are

The college was established in the academic year (2017-2018) to keep pace with the escalating and growing developments in the field of computer science and information technology, where the college offers a bachelor's degree program in the specialties of (computer science, information technology and software). The College of computer science and information technology is one of the active colleges at the local and regional levels in providing the labor market with qualified and specialists capable of keeping up with the escalating changes and requirements in the field of information technology. The college is considered an incubator for many research and applied innovations and achievements that serve the level of the educational process at the university.

Objectives.

  • Providing the student with modern knowledge in the field of computer science and information technology.
  • Preparing a distinguished information society through the graduation of scientific cadres specialized in the fields of computer.
  • Training students on teamwork, applied research and innovative creativity.
  • Carrying out scientific studies and research required by the labor market and participating in providing effective solutions to real problems in order to advance the development process..
